Our Story

From humble beginnings in Tokyo to sharing authentic ramen with the world.

The Beginning

In 1985, Master Chef Hiroshi Tanaka opened a small ramen shop in the bustling streets of Shibuya, Tokyo. With a passion for preserving traditional Japanese flavors and techniques passed down through his family for generations, he dedicated his life to perfecting the art of ramen making.

Growing Recognition

Word of mouth spread quickly about the exceptional quality and authentic taste of Sakura Ramen. Local food critics and international visitors alike praised the restaurant's commitment to traditional methods, from the 24-hour bone broth preparation to the handmade noodles crafted daily.

Global Expansion

By 2010, demand for authentic Sakura Ramen had grown beyond Tokyo. We began our franchise program to share our passion for traditional Japanese ramen with communities worldwide, while maintaining the strict quality standards and authentic recipes that made us famous.
